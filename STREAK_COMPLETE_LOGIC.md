# 🔥 Complete Streak Logic - Streaker App

## 📋 **What Counts for a Streak?**

To maintain or earn a streak, you need to complete **4 CORE GOALS** daily:

### ✅ **Required Goals (Must Complete All 4)**

1. **📱 Steps Goal**
   - **Target**: 10,000 steps/day (customizable)
   - **Required**: ≥80% (8,000+ steps)
   - **Why**: Movement is essential for health

2. **🍔 Calorie Goal**
   - **Target**: 2,000 kcal/day (customizable)
   - **Required**: ≤120% (up to 2,400 kcal)
   - **Why**: Balanced eating without being too restrictive

3. **😴 Sleep Goal**
   - **Target**: 8 hours/night (customizable)
   - **Required**: ≥80% (6.4+ hours)
   - **Why**: Recovery is crucial for fitness

4. **🍽️ Nutrition Logging**
   - **Target**: Log at least 1 meal
   - **Required**: Any calories logged (>0)
   - **Why**: Awareness of food intake

### 💧 **Optional Goal (Bonus Points)**
- **Water Intake**: 8 glasses/day
- Tracked for health but NOT required for streak
- Shows in progress percentage as extra achievement

## 🛡️ **Grace Period System (Life Happens!)**

### 2-Day Safety Net
You get **2 consecutive "excuse days"** where you can miss your goals without losing your streak!

**How it works:**
- **Day 1 Miss**: Streak protected (1 grace day used)
- **Day 2 Miss**: Still safe (2 grace days used)
- **Day 3 Miss**: Streak resets to 0 😢
- **Complete Goals**: Grace period resets to full 2 days

### Examples:
```
✅ Monday: Complete all 4 goals (Streak: 5, Grace: 0/2)
❌ Tuesday: Busy day, missed goals (Streak: 5, Grace: 1/2) ⚠️
❌ Wednesday: Still recovering (Streak: 5, Grace: 2/2) ⚠️⚠️
✅ Thursday: Back on track! (Streak: 6, Grace: 0/2) 🎉
```

## 📊 **Goal Thresholds (80% Rule)**

The app uses an **80% completion threshold** to make streaks achievable:

| Goal | 100% Target | 80% Threshold | Example |
|------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Steps | 10,000 | 8,000+ | Walk 8,500 = ✅ |
| Sleep | 8 hours | 6.4+ hours | Sleep 7 hours = ✅ |
| Calories | 2,000 max | 2,400 max | Eat 2,200 = ✅ |
| Food Log | Log meals | Any logged | 1 meal logged = ✅ |

## 🔄 **When Does Streak Update?**

### Automatic Sync Points:
1. **App Open**: Checks and syncs immediately
2. **Health Data Sync**: When HealthKit/Health Connect updates
3. **Manual Entry**: After logging food/water/weight
4. **Background Sync**: Every 5 minutes when app is active
5. **Midnight Reset**: Daily at 12:00 AM local time

### Real-time Updates:
- Changes sync to Supabase cloud instantly
- Multi-device support (same streak on all devices)
- Offline mode caches data, syncs when online

## ❓ **FAQ**

**Q: What if I exercise but don't hit 8,000 steps?**
A: Steps are just one metric. Consider adjusting your step goal in settings if you do other exercises.

**Q: Can I lose grace days permanently?**
A: No! Grace days reset to full 2 days every time you complete your goals.

**Q: Does the streak reset at midnight?**
A: The day resets at midnight local time, but you have until 11:59 PM to complete goals.

**Q: What if my device doesn't sync?**
A: You can manually enter data in the app. Tap the + buttons on each metric card.

**Q: Can I pause my streak for vacation?**
A: Currently no pause feature, but the 2-day grace period helps with short breaks.
